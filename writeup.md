### Abstract
The purpose of my implementation is to exploit known properties of a given matrix, **A** of size  <a href="https://www.codecogs.com/eqnedit.php?latex=\mathbb{R}^{ndxnd}" target="_blank"><img src="https://latex.codecogs.com/gif.latex?\mathbb{R}^{ndxnd}" title="\mathbb{R}^{ndxnd}" /></a>, and perform efficient matrix operations.  The solution enables operations of **A** on large values of *n* and *d* by first creating a compact represenation for **A**.
